Frequently Asked Questions

Every business (a small, medium or large enterprise) can benefit from the Zillancer Support Portal. A business with a suitable, well maintained and supported ERP System will see a greater return on investment and growth in their business.

Step 1: Click on the "Sign up" button, which is located in the upper right corner of the homepage.
Step 2: Fill in your personal information, including your name, mobile number, email address, and password.
Step 3: After filling in your details, you’ll need to verify your email address.
Here’s how:
  • Check your email inbox. You should have received an email from Zillancer Support Portal (check your spam/junk folder if it's not in your inbox).
  • In the email, you will find a verification link. Click on this link.
  • Your browser will open, and you will be directed to a confirmation page on the Zillancer Support Portal website.
  • Your email address is now verified, and you can log in to your Zillancer Support Portal account.
Step 4: Once you’ve verified your email, it’s important to provide essential details in your profile, such as your company name, industry, the ERP software you use, and more. Your profile must be 100% complete to begin purchasing packages and generating tickets.

Although the time might differ from user to user, for overall completion, it takes the average user around 3 to 5 minutes to complete. Note that you need to complete your profile 100% before you will be able to proceed.

Step 1: Visit the Zillancer Support Portal website and log in to your account. If you don’t have an account, create one to access the Zillancer Support Portal.
Step 2: On your dashboard, click on the dropdown menu located in the upper right corner. Select "Packages" from the menu. You will be redirected to the packages page where you can choose a subscription that aligns with your needs.
Step 3: Browse the available support packages and select on the package that best suits your needs:

  • Monthly Package – Ideal for time-intensive assistance.
  • Quarterly Package – Suitable for medium-sized projects over a longer period.
  • Annual Package – The All-Star Package for complex, time-intensive tasks.
Step 4: Once you've selected the package that aligns with your requirements, click on "Buy Now." You will be securely redirected to the payment page, where you can enter your payment information and billing details.

After you have purchased a package, your "available hours" will reflect in your wallet immediately.

The process is simple, with a quick turn-around time. If you are happy with the estimated time and expert ERP consultant assigned, the project can start immediately.

You can easily add more hours to your wallet if the project requires additional time. This adjustment takes effect immediately, allowing the project to resume without interruption.

Once the Admin assigns your ticket to an expert ERP consultant and receives the time estimation from the ERP consultant, you will receive a notification with the details. You can then choose to accept or reject. If you are satisfied and would like to proceed, accept the ticket. If you are not satisfied with the time estimation, you can reject the ticket and then message the Admin to re-assign your project to another expert ERP consultant.

Please Note: In order to create a support ticket, you must first have an active support package.
Step 1: Start by visiting the Zillancer Support Portal website and logging in to your account.
Step 2: After logging in, navigate to the “New Ticket” section on your account dashboard. Click on it to begin the ticket creation process.
Step 3: In the support ticket form, concisely state the problem you’re facing with your ERP system. It’s essential to provide important details and any relevant information to help our experts understand your issue effectively.
Step 4: If you have any supporting documents or files related to your issue, you can attach them to your support ticket to provide more context.
Step 5: Before finalizing, review the information you’ve provided in the ticket form to ensure accuracy and completeness. Click the “Submit” button to send your support request. Once you've submitted your ticket, you will receive a confirmation email from us.
